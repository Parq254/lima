Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lima?

Lima is an online agriculture e-commerce platform that provides farmers with essential farm items on loan. Our unique model allows farmers to pay for these items using their crop output.

How does the Loan process work?

Farmers can select the farm items they need from our platform. These items are provided on loan, and farmers agree to pay for these items using their crop output. The specifics of this agreement are outlined in a separate Loan Agreement.

How do I make a payment using my crop output?

The specifics of the payment process are outlined in the Loan Agreement. Generally, farmers agree to allocate a portion of their crop output as payment for the loaned items.

What happens if my crop output is not enough to cover the cost of the loaned items?

We understand that farming can be unpredictable. In the event that a farmer's crop output is not sufficient to cover the cost of the loaned items, we encourage the farmer to contact us to discuss possible solutions.

How can I contact Lima?

You can contact us via email or phone. Our customer service team is always ready to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

What types of farm items do you offer?

We offer a wide range of farm items, including seeds, fertilizers, and farming equipment. Our goal is to provide farmers with the resources they need to increase their productivity.
